The Artificial Intelligence Services Organization
Discussing "The Social Dilemma" and concsious innovation has given me new perspective on what type of company to join. I knew I wanted to joing a company that "does something good", but defining "good" as driving free agency, inclusivity, and well-being is a helpful framework to work with. I will also be keenly aware of how easy it is to ignore the hard questions about the future that should never be left unexplored.

Innovation postures are also helpful when determining an organization to join. Do I want to join a culture of innovation, or drive it from a small team? What are the real challenges ahead? How do I prevent my own organization from slipping into modes of low performance or "just the now"?
