Conscious Innovation
"Thinking small is a self-fulfilling prophecy", at least according to Amazon. But what happens when we go big by accident? Are our products ready for an impact that reaches millions or even billions of people?

Deep Learning Neural Networks develop solutions to questions based on provided datasets. While this avoids many decision traps that humans are prone to, it still poses a risk of sustaining or amplifying biases.
Artificial Intelligence takes on the biases inherent in datasets. The logic within a neural net is not transparent to humans, which makes rooting out these biases a challenge. AI creators must boldly question the potential unintended outcomes of their product, and make swift corrections.
It is incumbent upon Artificial Intelligence developers to anticipate and test for harmful outcomes. To be complacent is to be complicit.
